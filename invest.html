Q & A


How long will my package last?

The time of your package is instantly set to thirty days (one month), but we could make an exception on demand. If you would like to make an exception, please Contact Us or send us an email at UGA@gmail.com


Can i withdraw a part of my funds before the investment project runs out?

You cannot access your fund once they have been invested in a package. They are perceived to be in growth. Altering this process might corrupt the simple interest algorithms. Unless, you want to abort the package completely - you could do this by clicking on the abort button on the bottom-right of the balance report sheet in the Finance web-page.


Can I demand for a refund?

You could request for a refund of the money you used in purchasing the package, if it has not yet been activated in the space of 7days. You could also get your deposits refunded as long as it is in alliance with our refund policies.
